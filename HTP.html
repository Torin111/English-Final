Backstory:

The Copper Key awaits explorers
In a Tomb Filled with Horrors
But you have much to learn
If you hope to earn
A place among the high scorers
You are Wade Watts, a boy who lives in the stacks of Chicago. However, inside the OASIS, you are known as Parzival. The OASIS is basically the simulation that everyone spends most of their time in. Ever since James Halliday, the creator of the OASIS, died, you have been trying to find the easter egg that he hid inside the OASIS, which would give you access to Halliday's considerable fortune.
